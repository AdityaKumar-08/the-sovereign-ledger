Migration Crisis

Bihar is India's largest source of inter-state migrants. Approximately 3.5M workers/year migrate to Delhi, Mumbai, Punjab, and Haryana for employment.

2051 Outlook: Reverse migration expected as GSDP grows. Patna corridor may become a major employment hub.
Source: Census Migration Tables, NSSO PLFS
